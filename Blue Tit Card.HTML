Blue Tit

Adults have white cheeks, but juveniles have yellow.

  • Scientific Name: Cyanistes caeruleus
  • Average Length: 12 cm
  • Wingspan: 13 cm
  • Migration: Non Migratory

The Blue Tit is commonly seen at bird feeders in the UK and Ireland. Sexes are similar. The blue cap differentiates the Blue Tit from others in the Tit family.
