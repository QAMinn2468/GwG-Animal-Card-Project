Sandhill Crane

Over 600,000 Sandhill Cranes migrate on the Platte River valley each year from late February to early April in order to "fuel up".

  • Scientific Name: Grus canadensis
  • Length: 34-48 inches
  • Wingspan: 73-90 inches
  • Migration: Migratory

Monogamous. Roosts communally in winter, standing on damp low land, or in shallow water. Courtship dances in late winter and spring involve bounding 6-8 feet into the air with wings half spread and calling loudly.
